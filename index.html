Octopus Species

Blue-ringed octopus

The blue-ringed octopi (genus Hapalochlaena) are three (or perhaps four) octopus species that live in tide pools and coral reefs in the Pacific and Indian Oceans, from Japan to Australia.

California two-spot octopus

The California two-spot octopus (Octopus bimaculoides) is an octopus species found off the coast of California.

Giant Pacific

Enteroctopus dofleini, also known as the giant Pacific octopus or North Pacific giant octopus, is a large marine cephalopod belonging to the genus Enteroctopus. Its spatial distribution includes California, Oregon, Washington, British Columbia, Alaska, Russia, northern Japan, and Korea.[1]
